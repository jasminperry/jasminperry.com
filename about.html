About

Jasmin is a writer from Somerset.

She graduated with an MA in Creative Writing from Bath Spa University in 2020. Her work has been published in the RSPB’s Back from the Brink anthology, the Untitled:Voices journal, The Everyday magazine and Rife magazine, amongst others. In 2019 she founded Weston Writers’ Nights, an organisation supporting writers through writing events, workshops and a printed magazine, and was listed as one of Rife magazine’s ‘24 Most Influential Bristolians Under 24’.

She is currently working on her debut novel.
